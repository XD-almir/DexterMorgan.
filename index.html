Dexter Morgan

The story of a forensic expert with a dark secret.

Dexter Morgan is a blood spatter analyst for Miami Metro Police Department by day, and a vigilante serial killer by night. Guided by a strict moral code, Dexter only targets criminals who have escaped justice. Torn between his human side and his dark urges, Dexter's story is one of complexity and suspense.
